About

I'm currently a Mechanical Engineering student at Northeastern University, Boston MA. I'm strongly interested in robotics, and I hope to work more with systems and mechatronics. My interest in robotics/systems comes from my love for sci-fi. Tony Stark/Iron Man was a big reason why I chose ME as my career, and I want to be able to bring sci-fi tech to reality in my freetime, such as Jetpacks, Dum-E robots, Ligthsabers, and Exoskeletons. Influencers like the HackSmith and JLaserVideo inspire me as a mechanical engineer to be able to make similar gadgets in my freetime. In order to make these gadgets, I thought that having a systems understanding of engineering would be the most helpful, combining mechanical, electrical, and software engineering.
