About

I am currently a Mechanical Engineering undergraduate student at Northeastern University in Boston, MA. I am strongly interested in robotics, and I hope to gain more real-world experience working with robotics and systems. I also plan to pursue an MSME focused on robotics or mechatronics.

My interest in robotics and systems engineering comes from my love of sci-fi. Tony Stark (Iron Man) was a big reason I chose Mechanical Engineering back in high school, and this passion has developed into a desire to recreate sci-fi technology in real life. Creators like the Hacksmith and JLaservideo inspire me through their recreations of jetpacks, lightsabers, holograms, wall climbers, and more. To make this possible, I am building a strong understanding of systems engineering, combining mechanical, electrical, and software engineering.
