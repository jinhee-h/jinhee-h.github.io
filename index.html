About Me

I’m a third-year Mechanical Engineering student at Northeastern University. I'm currently open to exploring career opportunities across various ME fields and industries to understand the work, people, and environment that fit me best.

I was fortunate to complete a six-month co-op at SharkNinja, where I collaborated with fellow co-ops and cross-functional teams to experience how an engineering team functions in a fast-paced corporate setting. I was able to see a product's lifecycle from concept development to mass production. I gained invaluable hands-on knowledge, but my proudest takeaway was the growth of my engineering mindset: learning to anticipate potential issues, critically analyze system-level problems, and approach solutions from creative angles.
